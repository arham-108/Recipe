Garlic Pasta Recipe

Ingredients

  • 200g (7 oz) pasta (spaghetti, fettuccine, or your choice)
  • 3 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Black pepper (to taste)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil (optional, helps prevent sticking)
  • 2 tbsp pasta water (reserved)
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (optional garnish)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Instructions

Boil The Pasta

  1. Fill a large pot with water and bring it to a boil.
  2. Add a big pinch of salt (about 1–2 tsp).
  3. Add 200g of pasta to the boiling water.
  4. Cook according to the package instructions (usually 8–10 minutes) until al dente (firm to the bite).
  5. Reserve 2 tablespoons of pasta water before draining.
  6. Drain the pasta and set aside.
Make Garlic Butter Sauce
  1. In a large skillet or pan, melt 3 tbsp of butter over medium heat.
  2. Add the minced garlic and sauté for 1–2 minutes until fragrant and lightly golden (don’t burn it!).
  3. (Optional) Add a splash of olive oil if you like it a bit richer.
Add the cooked pasta to the skillet with the garlic butter.
  1. Pour in the reserved pasta water — this helps make the sauce cling to the pasta.
  2. Toss everything together for 1–2 minutes until well-coated.
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
Serve
  1. Plate The Pasta
  2. (Optional) Sprinkle with chopped parsley and grated Parmesan.
Serve Hot And Enjoy!!!
